## About me

I'm a computer engineer and founder who enjoys turning real product problems into maintainable software.

I currently build and operate **Zepliner**, an eSIM platform with mobile apps, a web application, backend services, payment infrastructure, and partner integrations. My work spans product development, system design, infrastructure, deployment, monitoring, and the less glamorous operational details required to keep software running in production.

My academic background includes low-level programming, computer architecture, embedded systems, algorithms, applied mathematics, and computer vision. These days, most of my work is focused on backend engineering, cloud infrastructure, developer tooling, and end-to-end product systems.

> [!NOTE]
> Much of my production work lives in private repositories. My public repositories mainly contain selected projects, engineering notes, experiments, and learning work.

## Current focus

- Building and improving Zepliner across backend, web, iOS, Android, and partner APIs
- Designing reliable APIs, deployment workflows, and cloud infrastructure
- Documenting engineering decisions, technical notes, and selected projects
- Deepening my knowledge of system design, algorithms, databases, and distributed systems
